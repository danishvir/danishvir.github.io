Computer History in the 1990s

What key events occured in computer history during the 1990s?

1. Sergey Brin and Larry Page co-found Google in 1998.

As part of a research project at Stanford University, Sergey Brin and Larry Page co-found the Google search engine on September 4, 1998 in Menlo Park, California.

2. In 1999, Wi-Fi becomes a part of the computer language.

Wi-Fi becomes a part of the computer language and people start connecting to the Internet without using wires.

3. In 1990, HML is invented.

Tim Berners-Lee invents HTML (HyperText Markup Language).This gave rise to the World Wide Web.

4.The Pentium microprocessor advances the use of graphics and music on PCs.

The Pentium microprocessor was made in 1993,advancing the use of graphics and music on PCs.It was introduced as a successor to Intel's 80486 microprocessor.

5.In 1994, PCs start becoming gaming machines as new PC games are released.

New PC games start to get released, including Command and Conquer,Alone in the Dark 2, and Magic Carpet, among others.
