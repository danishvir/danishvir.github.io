Computer History in the 2010s

What key events occured in computer history during the 2010s?

1. The Adobe Creative Cloud is announced in 2011.

In 2011, Adobe Creative Cloud was announced as the subscription and cloud-based model for its products. Acrobat, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, and Acrobat were included in it.

2. Siri is announced.

In 2011, Siri was added as a built-in fe that came with the Apple iPhone 4s smartphone in October.It could understand natural language requests, something not done before.

3.Nest Learning thermostat is introduced.

The Nest Learning Thermostat is a smart thermostat invented by Nest Labs and designed by Tony Fadell, Ben Filson, and Fred Bould.Its current cost is $229.

4.Apple Watch is released. Apple Watch is released. It is basically a watch that can function as your phone. It is compatible with your iPhone.

5. The iPad is released.

The iPad is like the iPhone, but bigger.
