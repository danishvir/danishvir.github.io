Samsung Galaxy S9

On March 11, 2018, Samsung released the Samsung released the Samsung Galaxy S9. The Galaxy S9 is a very good phone. This phone has a large screen and also an amazing camera. Although this phones has not undergone major design changes-it looks exactly like 2017's S8- it has a better speaker(which is now a stereo).

Positive

Positive- The S9 has a bigger screen and a low-light camera. It also has a very good speaker. Another major highlight is the better-placed finger sensor. In the Samsung Galaxy S8, the fingerprint sensor was off center and right next to the camera lens on the back. However, Samsung fixed that problem this year. That is a major improvement and definitely something S8 users will appreciate.If you want to afford a phone within a price range of $700-$900, the S9 is definitely one of your best bets.

Negative

Negative- The S9 is a great phone.However,it has some problems as well, including the AR Emoji track and the Intelligent Scan, which both fall short.Also, the camera switches to easily among modes, which can become very frustrating.

Target Market

The target market is Millenials with the new smarter camera and new AR Emoji feature. Most of our campaigns are focused on those people entering the work market and want a phone that does everything they want it to do".

Also,Strothers claimed that the focus is also on female users. Personal Opinion

In my opinion, the S9 is worth buying, as it is relatively affordable and has some very nice features.
